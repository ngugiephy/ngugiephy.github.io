About the pico-W

RP2040 is a debut microcontroller from the Raspberry Pi. It is a low-cost, high-performance microcontroller device with flexible digital interfaces.

Its key features include:

  • Dual Cortex M0+ processor cores, up to 133MHz
  • 264kB of embedded SRAM in 6 banks
  • 26 multifunction GPIO
  • 6 dedicated IO for SPI Flash (supporting XIP)
  • Dedicated hardware for commonly used peripherals
  • Programmable IO for extended peripheral support
  • 4 channel ADC with internal temperature sensor, 500ksps, 12-bit conversion
  • USB 1.1 Host/Device
